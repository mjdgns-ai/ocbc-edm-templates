FX CONTRACT BOOKING

With OCBC Velocity, you can book FX contracts online in just a few clicks. Lock in live rates across 15 major currencies, and use your contracts seamlessly for international transfers.

 

GET STARTED ON OCBC VELOCITY

How to book an FX contract

1
Log in to OCBC Velocity, select “FX & Treasury” followed by “Book FX Contract”.
2
Choose your currency pair and enter the amount, then select “Get rate” to view the live FX rate. Confirm the transaction to lock in the rate.
3
Receive your confirmation instantly. View and manage your contracts under “FX Trade History”.
 
OR
 
 

GET STARTED ON THE OCBC BUSINESS APP

How to book an FX contract on mobile

1
Log in to the OCBC Business app and select “Book FX Contract”.
2
Enter your amount, select your currency pair and value date, then tap “Get rate” to view and lock in the live rate.
3
Receive your confirmation and use the contract on the value date for your international transfer.
