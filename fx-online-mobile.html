GET STARTED ON VELOCITY

How to book an FX contract

1
Log in to OCBC Velocity, select “FX & Treasury” then “Book FX Contract”.
2
Choose your currency pair and amount, then tap “Get rate” to view and lock in the live rate.
3
Receive your confirmation instantly and manage contracts under “FX Trade History”.
 
OR
 
 

VIA THE BUSINESS APP

Book FX on the go

1
Log in to the OCBC Business app and select “Book FX Contract”.
2
Enter your amount, currency pair and value date. Tap “Get rate” to view and lock in the live rate.
3
Receive your confirmation and use the contract on the value date for your transfer.
