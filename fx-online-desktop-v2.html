HOW IT WORKS

Book an FX contract in 3 steps

1
Log in to OCBC Velocity, select “FX & Treasury” followed by “Book FX Contract”.
2
Choose your currency pair and amount, then select “Get rate” to view the live rate. Confirm to lock it in.
3
Done. Receive your confirmation instantly and manage contracts under “FX Trade History”.

Prefer mobile? The same 3 steps work on the OCBC Business app — just tap “Book FX Contract” to get started.
