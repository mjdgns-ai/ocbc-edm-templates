  • Hook quantity depends on your product spacing — typical layout is 6-8 hooks per 560mm panel row per bay.
Assembly

Assembly Sequence

1

Install Feet on Posts

Slide 400mm feet onto the base of each of the 3 double-sided posts using the turn-lock tool and soft mallet. Each post gets 2 feet (front and back).

2

Connect Posts with Joining Rails

Attach 2 joining rails (top) and 2 base joining rails (bottom) between the 3 posts to form the 2-bay frame. Use the turn-lock tool.

3

Install Pegboard Panels

Slide pegboard panels into post slots on both sides: two 560mm-high panels and one 240mm-high panel per bay per side (filling below the demo shelf area). Total: 8 large + 4 small panels.

4

Mount Demo Shelves

Hook the 30mm shelf bracket sets into the post slots near the top (both sides) and place the metal shelves on the brackets. Attach shelf lips to the front edge to prevent items from sliding off.

5

Add Finishing Touches

Install metal skirts at floor level, top capping on the post tops, and arrange your pegboard hooks across the panels at desired spacing.

6

Attach Magnetic Lock-Stops

Fit magnetic security locks to hook tips to prevent unauthorized removal of hanging boxed products.

Tools Required

  • Turn-lock tool (E0001.4) — included in order
  • Soft faced mallet (E0005.4) — included in order
  • Spirit level (bring your own)
  • No drilling required — this is a freestanding unit
